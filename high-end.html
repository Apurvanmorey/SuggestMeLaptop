What we should expect Under ₹70,000?

You will find many laptops within this price range, but you should be looking for a device with Full HD screen with decent viewing angles also at least Dual core CPU (Quad Core is better) if you want decent graphics profermence then AMD Ryzen CPU with Vega Series iGPU's. For storage, you can expect 256gb SSD or a Terabyte of Hard Drive Space.
